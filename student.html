Welcome to the Computer Science Department

Empowering Minds, Shaping the Future

Welcome to the vibrant community of the University of Ghana Computer Science Department! As a hub of innovation and learning, we are dedicated to shaping future leaders and contributors to the dynamic field of computer science.

Our department is committed to providing a world-class education, fostering creativity, and preparing students for exciting careers in technology. Whether you are just starting your academic journey or looking to advance your skills, we offer a diverse range of programs and resources to support your success.

Explore our courses, engage with our faculty, and discover the opportunities that await you in the ever-evolving world of computer science. Join us as we embark on a journey of exploration, discovery, and excellence!

Student Life

Clubs and Organizations

Immerse yourself in the vibrant student life of the Computer Science Department. Joining clubs and organizations is a fantastic way to connect with fellow students who share your passion for technology and innovation.

COMPSSA - Computer Science Students Association

COMPSSA is the heart of our student community. As a member, you'll have the opportunity to participate in tech events, workshops, and collaborative library. It's a great platform to network with your peers and stay updated on the latest trends in the tech industry.

Google Developers Group - University of Ghana

Join the Google Developers Group to engage with like-minded students interested in Google technologies. Attend hackathons, coding competitions, and tech talks organized by the group. Enhance your skills and explore Google's latest innovations.

Huawei Ghana Tech Club

Be part of the Huawei Ghana Tech Club and delve into the world of cutting-edge technologies. Gain insights into Huawei's advancements in the tech industry through workshops, seminars, and hands-on library.

Sports

Embrace the spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition by getting involved in sports activities at the Computer Science Department. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just looking to have some fun, there's a place for you in our sports community.

Join COMPSSA in gearing up for the upcoming African Games happening in March 2024, hosted by the University of Ghana. As we proudly represent our department, it's not just about the games; it's an opportunity to foster teamwork, build lasting friendships, and showcase the athletic prowess of our computer science community.

Come be a part of the excitement, cheering for our teams and celebrating the spirit of sportsmanship!

Physical Resources

Explore the rich physical resources available to Computer Science students, designed to enhance your learning experience and provide a conducive environment for academic growth.

Our state-of-the-art computer labs are equipped with the latest technology, providing hands-on experience for coding, software development, and research library.

For those seeking a quiet space for studying and research, the Balme Library is a treasure trove of knowledge. With an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources, it's the perfect place to delve into the world of computer science.

Additionally, the Computer Science Library, tailored to the specific needs of our department, offers a specialized collection, including textbooks, reference materials, and computer resources to support your academic journey.

Support Services

Health Services

At the University of Ghana's Computer Science Department, your well-being is our priority. The university's health services offer comprehensive medical care to students, including access to the university hospital for any health-related needs.

In addition to the university's health facilities, our department also provides first aid services within the department premises. Your safety and health are paramount to us, and we are committed to ensuring a supportive and healthy learning environment.

Career Guidance

Embarking on a journey in computer science requires not only academic excellence but also strategic career planning. Our dedicated team of course advisors, including the hardworking Mark Attah and other esteemed lecturers, is here to provide personalized career guidance.

Whether you're exploring internship opportunities, preparing for job interviews, or seeking advice on career paths in the field of computer science, our advisors are ready to assist you. Take advantage of our career guidance services to make informed decisions and shape a successful future in the dynamic world of technology.

News and Updates

Stay informed with the latest happenings and updates from the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the classroom, and we ensure you are well-informed about important events, announcements, and opportunities.

Explore the world of technology through our news updates, featuring achievements of our students, faculty, and the department as a whole. Whether it's groundbreaking research, student accomplishments, or upcoming events, this section is your gateway to the vibrant and dynamic community within the Computer Science Department.
