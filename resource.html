Undergraduate Degree Programmes

The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes:

  1. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Single Major
  2. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Major Minor
  3. Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Admission Requirements

To qualify for admission into Computer Science programmes at Level 200, candidates must have taken the following courses at Level 100:

  • MATH 121: Algebra and Trigonometry
  • MATH 122: Calculus 1
  • CSCD 101: Introduction to Computer Science I
  • CSCD 102: Introduction to Computer Science II

Laptop Requirement

Every student in the Department of Computer Science must own a laptop with the following minimum specifications:

  • i3 64-bit Processor
  • 8GB RAM
  • 500GB HD or 256 SSD
